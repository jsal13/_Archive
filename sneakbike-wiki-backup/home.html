What is Sneakbike?

Sneakbike is a community of people working towards two primary goals:

  • Community: Making friends, being nice to each other, and friendly competition.
  • Learning New Skills: A commitment to helping each other grow and learn new things.

What does Sneakbike do?

The Sneakbike Community currently hosts two different events: Sneakbike Mystery Races and Sneakly Weeklys.

Sneakbike Mystery Races

Mystery Races are events where four similarly-skilled players race to complete an objective in one, two,or three "mystery games" which are only revealed to the runners a few minutes before the race starts!

Sneakly Weeklys

This is the Sneakbike Community's "anything goes" event. It's a good time to try out some new event ideas or to have a community night.

In the past we've had non-mystery races, casual playthroughs of weird games, learning a speedrun together, teaching streams, learning randomizer games, hollow knight randomizer bingo, and sometimes we just hang out and play games together.
