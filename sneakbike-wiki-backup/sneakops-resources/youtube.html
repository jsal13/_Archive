Sneakbike has a Youtube channel , which we use to upload our VODs of our past Twitch broadcasts. We do a few edits on them before publishing them.

Upload Twitch VOD to Youtube

By default, uploaded videos are private until you publish them. You can always check the visibility of each video while in Youtube Studio mode and under the tab “Content."

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Edit the title & description

Hover over the video you wish to edit and click on the pencil icon that says ‘Details’. This will take you to the video details page. We have a canned title & description that will automatically fill for every upload (which can be changed in Youtube Studio > Settings > Upload Defaults). 

Titles

  • Mystery Races: currently, the default title is formatted for a mystery race: “Sneakbike Retro Mystery Race (Console): GAME #1, GAME #2". Update the console, keeping it in parentheses, and add the titles of the games played.
  • Special Events: there's no set format for special events like tournaments and Sneakly Weeklies; use your best judgement. Things like dates and racers in the title are not important.
